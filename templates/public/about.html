The Atlas QMS

A digital quality system, end-to-end.

Our electronic QMS (eQMS) digitises every step of the quality lifecycle — from AI-powered complaint triage to real-time CAPA tracking, SPC dashboards, and external lab partner coordination. Every quality event is captured, analysed, and resolved with full audit-trail visibility.

  • Stage-based batch tracking — IQC, IPQC, and FQC gates with electronic sign-off.
  • Statistical Process Control — Live control charts with automatic out-of-spec flagging.
  • External lab tracker — Manage FQC samples sent to accredited partners in real time.
  • CAPA workflow — Root-cause analysis and preventive-action tracking for every complaint.
